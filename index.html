Welcome to the Bronze VIP Posting Board Archives. These pages collect the posts of VIP Users of the various posting boards which cover our favorite Mutant Enemy shows--Buffy, Angel, and Firefly

Prior to July 10, 2001, that would have been the Bronze at the WB's Buffy the Vampire Slayer site. Once Buffy moved to UPN, however, a slew of new posting boards showed up, including the Bronze Beta. For the most part, you'll find that most VIPs will post there, but I do tend to find the odd posts from at the old UPN Bronze, and some Firefly fan sites.

"I find it very useful."

--Tim Minear on the Bronze VIP Posting Board Archives
(Gratuitous Celebrity Endorsement).

These pages, unless otherwise indicated, archive the posting board as they are--more recent posts are included at the top of the page--to read in sequential order, go to the bottom of the page, and scroll up. If my scripts haven't found a VIP in a particular day's postings, it shouldn't list that day.

While I try to fix some of the HTML tags that I retrieve from the posting board, some errors may creep through. So if a page looks funky in all italics or if the text keeps growing smaller and smaller, or bolder and bolder, it's not my bad! ;) E-mail me with the page URL and the problem you're seeing.

Thanks to everyone who's given me feedback on the site. If I neglect to return your mail, I apologize--I usually end up fixing whatever problem indicated and forgetting to reply to the generous person who pointed the problem out.

Note: These pages may contain spoilers for episodes you haven't seen. Read at your own risk. Spoilers, misinformation, and other aromatic herbs are not my responsibility.

If you notice that I've missed some VIP postings (or if you want to offer some comments or criticism), please feel free to send me some e-mail, and tell me the VIP's name as well as the date and time that they posted.
