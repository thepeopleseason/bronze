Beldin: You don't know 42nd Street? Well, it's a Broadway musical based on the 1933 movie of the same name. It opened on B'way in 1980 starring Jerry Orbach and Tammy Grimes (in the role I'll be playing) when it won the Tony for Best Musical. Then, there was a revival in 2001 starring Christine Ebersole (again in the role I'll be playing), and it won Tony's for Best Revival of a Musical and also Best Actress in a Musical. It's a backstage musical. Lots of dancing, particularly big tap numbers. (But not from me!) My character's the big diva, who eventually breaks her ankle, which forces the young innocent chorus girl, Peggy Sawyer, to go on for her and save the show. The famous line, "You're gonna go out there a youngster and come back a star!" is from 42nd Street. Very cute stuff.
