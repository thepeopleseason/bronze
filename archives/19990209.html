Meeb & KrazyKat - All of the stunts on tonights episode I've used on Buffy before. The final spin and crash onto the box was in the "hellfight." Joss and I had talked about moving the camera around to shoot it properly that day, but we didn't have the time. Since Joss liked it I put it the warehouse at the end and had the same stuntman (Chris O'Hara) do it again. He told me later that he dug it.

The one thing that was different was the bit where Angel throws the guy into a side spin. Normally I call it a Hong Kong Spin (because Jackie Chan did it first) but the HK guys call it a 720. This time I wrapped a wire around Lee Whitaker (the Oz Stunt Double here playing a Vamp) and gave him a couple of extra turns in order to show how strong this Angel dude can be. I felt that he needed to really wallop somebody since he hasn't done that in a while.
