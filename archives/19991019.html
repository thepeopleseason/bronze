Gosh... so many things to defend! I'm tired so I'll try to do it fast.

Buffy all wimpy: If she can deal with every problem easily there's no show. It would get boring to watch her kick various butts without getting to live though her emotional highs and lows... and for that you need lows. She's got super strength, but her heart is as fragile as any of ours.

Angel rescuing only women... hmm... interesting point about which I can't think of anything to say.

Endings of the eps seeming "off" Gosh, I hope not. I know the final shot of Buffy never looked exactly like we wanted... the point was supposed to be three different women all in the same emotional place due to bumps in the road to love. Did that not come through?

Spike all wimpy: You're kidding, right? He fought, he yelled, he abused his girlfriend, he tortured a guy... seemed scary to me!
