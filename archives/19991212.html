Okay... almost time to go. But first...

Hi Clover! Slave2Faith! Mr Pointy!

Yes, I'm coming to the PBP! I hope I'll see you all there! Fury is waffling on this point, you have to make him come too!

SLAYERplyr -- how does slaying demons made a person more socially comfortable in front of strangers? I don't see the connection. Nor do I see how it would keep your heart from being hurt by a charming young man who never called. Buffy is strong person precisely because she survives heartache... if she never *felt* heartache, well, then I'd say she wasn't human at all. Sorry if I sound strident, just a pet peeve of mine.

SlayedMyself asked about the hiring process. TV writers write "spec scripts" -- writing samples written for good shows. Their agents send the specs to Buffy (unless the spec is a Buffy -- no show reads specs of itself). The best of the specs are given to Joss to read and if he loves one he meets with the writer and listens to their Buffy ideas. Then he decides to hire them or not. My agent sent one of my (produced) Ellens and a (spec) NYPD Blue to Joss. I don't know how agents know which shows are looking for new writers. They just know.
