Argh. I wrote a long response and something happened to it. First of all, enthusiasm is never gushing, at least in my book. So, thanks for the wonderful enthusiasm.

Re. ideas: We work hard to come up with stuff that's interesting and hopefully fresh. I hope we succeed more often than not...

Writer's block: Deadlines are the cure, most of the time. If you don't have an externally imposed one (viz, from your editor), give yourself one. Or you might be blocked because you don't know what to do next. Ask yourself some questions, such as, what's the goal of this section?

Or you may be intimidated by the amount of writing you think you face. Break it down into smaller chunks. Set interim goals: "I'll write a page every day" or "I'll write for an hour."
