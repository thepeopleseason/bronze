Steve DeKnight says:
(Wed Feb 7 17:36:17 2001 216.186.167.165)
Okay, in no particular order:

Yes, I am indeed weevil. But I don't fall down (much).

Yes, I will be at the PBP. Wouldn't miss it. You glorious fans are what keep us going -- well, that and all the free candy.

Yes, I was a huge fan of BTVS before I was hired to write "Blood Ties". I was actually a fan even before I became a professional writer. Watched every episode and dreamed of what it'd be like to work on such an incredible show. Then I got hired on and oh let me tell you -- it's a zillion times better than I could ever have imagined. Everyone is so talented and funny and generous. It's amazing.

I'd like to take a minute to thank the cast and crew that brought "Blood Ties" to such vivid life. Sarah and Michelle's performances were gut-wrenching, even to the guy that wrote the script. And everyone else was equally amazing: Nicholas, Anthony, Alyson, Amber, James, Emma, Kristine, Clare, and Charlie -- big, gooey thanks for making my first episode so memorable. Also a huge thanks to the director, Michael Gershman. His talent is only surpassed by his kindness.

^
