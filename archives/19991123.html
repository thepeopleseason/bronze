Mallory, I believe, asked about the Indian tribe mentioned in today's ep. They were the Chumash, who lived in much of Southern California. The last full-blooded Chumash Indian died in 1952, although there is an active community of partial Chumash descendants.
They lived what is thought to have been a relatively peaceful life, harvesting fish and acorns. Lots and lots of acorns. They died by the tens of thousands in Spanish missions. It's all very sad. I did a lot of research, and YIKES.
