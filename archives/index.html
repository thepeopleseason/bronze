Past VIP Archives

Welcome to the Bronze VIP Posting Board Archives Archive. Since these pages are updated somewhat daily, my directories were getting quite full. The list of dates was also growing rather large, so I decided to rework the archives to make them a little more user-(or browser)-friendly.

Like the normal archives, these pages archive the posting board as they are--more recent posts are included at the top of the page--to read in sequential order, go to the bottom of the page, and scroll up. While most of us have seen every episode as they've come out, always beware of spoilers...
