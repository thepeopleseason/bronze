Jane Espenson says:
(Fri Oct 15 22:42:26 1999 152.163.197.193)

Ooh. An Ellen question... unusual! Yeah, I was there the season AFTER the coming out "puppy" episode. It was an interesting struggle because a 35-yr-old woman who just realizes that she's gay can't really NOT date... it would make the coming out meaningless, we felt. And, since the show had always suffered from the lack of a point of view, we took this new aspect of her life and just ran with it. In retrospect, maybe a mistake. But we wrote many episodes that I'm very proud of -- lots of funny, lots of truth.
And, by the way, I still maintain that episodes about her daily life with her girlfriend were no more about "gayness" than Mad About You was about "straightness."
So anyway, an interesting season right up 'til they yanked us off the air!
