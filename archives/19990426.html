Chris Beck (composer) says:
(Mon Apr 26 11:31:50 1999 209.178.16.205)

Driving by...

I've recently been contacted by Fox regarding the upcoming Buffy Soundtrack. Apparently TVT is considering putting one or two score pieces on it, and they asked me (through Fox) to submit possible selections for inclusion.

I mention this only because up until this point I have always been told there was no chance of there being any score on the CD. I wouldn't want to take anything away from Velvet Chain or Darling Violetta or Four Star Mary, all of whom in my opinion deserve a place on the CD, not to mention any number of the other Bronze bands. But if you want a score piece or two on the CD, now that I know it's a real possibility, I just wanted to ask those of you who are writing letters to TVT to add score to your list of requests. I'll need all the support I can get.

If they give me one track, it will probably be some newly-recorded version of the B/A love theme. If they give me two, I'll probably push for the ending bit from "The Wish" as a second track.

I also mention this because it means the final lineup is far from being set in stone. The list that was floating around a couple of weeks back seems quite tentative at this point. That is good news not only for me but also for the other Bronze bands who at this point have been left off the list.

Here again is the address:

Patricia Joseph
c/o TVT Records
23 E. 4th Street
3rd Floor
New York, NY 10003
re: Buffy Soundtrack

Thanks for all of your support!
- Chris
